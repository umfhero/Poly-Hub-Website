HOW IT WORKS

STEP 01

Create Your Profile

Launch PolyHub and create your profile. The app automatically detects your Tailscale IP and generates your identity. No account signup required.

STEP 02

Generate Pairing Link

Click to generate a pairing link containing your name, IP, and public key. Share this link with 1-2 trusted users via Discord, email, or any messaging app.

STEP 03

Share Files Instantly

Once connected, all users see the same shared gallery. Drag and drop files to share them instantly with everyone in the group. Files transfer directly peer-to-peer.

POWERED BY TAILSCALE

PolyHub requires Tailscale to create secure peer-to-peer connections. Tailscale provides encrypted mesh networking with automatic NAT traversal, making direct file transfers possible without port forwarding or complex network configuration.

  • End-to-end encrypted connections
  • Automatic NAT traversal (works behind firewalls)
  • Private mesh network (100.x.x.x IP range)
  • Zero-config peer discovery
